# Focus Buddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

## Overview

Focus Buddy is designed as a local-first focus timer. Your companion, settings, session history, streaks, and focus progress are stored on your device.

## Data Stored On Your Device

Focus Buddy stores app data locally on your device, including:

- Companion name, level, XP, happiness, and evolution progress
- Selected theme and app settings
- Timer settings and active timer recovery data
- Completed session history
- Task labels and session tags
- Daily focus goals, streaks, achievements, and focus statistics

## Data We Do Not Collect

Focus Buddy does not require an account, does not collect analytics, does not use advertising trackers, and does not send your focus data to an external server.

## Notifications

If you allow notifications, Focus Buddy uses them only to alert you when a focus session or break ends. You can disable notifications from your device settings at any time.

## Data Sharing

Focus Buddy does not sell, rent, or share your personal data. Because the app is local-only, your data stays on your device unless you choose to remove or transfer it yourself.

## Deleting Data

You can delete app data from the Settings screen with Reset App Data. You can also remove the app from your device to delete locally stored data.

## Changes

This policy may be updated when Focus Buddy adds new features. Material changes should be reflected in the app and store listing before release.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact listed on Focus Buddy's Google Play Store listing or open an issue in this repository.
